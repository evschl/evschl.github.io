What is a cube?

A cube is a curated draft environment where you pick cards one-by-one on the fly to craft a functional deck, then face off against the other people you drafted with.

The Niner Magic cube has a similar power level to most vintage cubes, with more of a focus on its creators' and the community's favorite archetypes.

Archetypes

| Weenies

Go wide and kill your opponent with an insurmountable band of small creatures!

| Control

Counter, tap, and return to hand your opponent's creatures, answering whatever they throw at you!

| Reanimator

Put powerful and impactful creatures into the graveyard and "cheat" them back out!

| Aggro

Kill your opponent quickly and ruthlessly before they get a chance to get on their feet!

| Ramp

Have bigger, better creatures than your opponents and swing into their feeble army!

| Mill

Your opponent's library size is their life total! Reduce their library size to zero and they're dead!

| Equipment

Suit your creatures up with flashy, powerful artifacts that enhance their combat abilities!

| Spellslinger

Play powerful, low-cost creatures and stifle your opponent's attempts to come back from your fast start!

| Counters

Play a bunch of smaller creatures and use +1/+1 counters to make them all huge!

| Blink

"Blink" your creatures in and out to re-trigger enters-the-battlefield abilities to bury your opponent in value!

| Lands

Use your lands as more than a mana resource to gain life, make tokens, and cast huge threats!

| Artifacts

Play cards that get more powerful the more artifacts you have, snowballing into a flashy win!

| Sacrifice

Sacrifice your creatures to make them more useful in death than in life! Well, you'll just raise them from the dead anyways. And then do it again!
