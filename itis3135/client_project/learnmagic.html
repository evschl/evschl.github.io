Learn Magic

So, you're interested in getting yourself or a friend into Magic™, but don't know where to start.

You probably fall into one of a few different categories already, so look for the section that best describes you!

"I've tried learning before, but I didn't really understand..."

You've tried before, but it didn't go so well...
A lot of us have gotten into Magic™ after the first try failed, as starting out can be a difficult undertaking.
There are so many different ways to play, and so many intricacies to playing at a higher level.
At its core however, Magic™ is very easy to pick up, understand, and figure out as you go!
The trick is to lay the foundation, and not to get into the difficult stuff right off the bat.
